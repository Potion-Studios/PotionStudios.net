Oh The Biomes We've Gone

Oh The Biomes We've Gone is the sequel to the timeless Oh The Biomes You'll Go mod for 1.20.1+ with over 50 breathtaking magical and realistic biomes. BWG will take you on an adventure by adding diverse and detailed life to Minecraft. BWG also adds new unique mobs, structures, and hundreds of blocks to utilize.

Oh The Biomes You'll Go

Oh The Biomes You'll Go is an exploration and adventure mod designed to take you on a road trip across Minecraft like no other! Explore over 80 magical, realistic, and breathtaking biomes.

All The Wood We've Got

All The Wood We've Got is a Side mod for Oh The Biomes We've Gone that adds in More Wood Block Variants. Such as Chests, Trapped Chests, Ladders and Barrels. With more Planned!
